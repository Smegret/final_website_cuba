Can Americans travel to Cuba?
The short answer is - YES! Although travel to Cuba for tourist activities is still prohibited, many are now able to travel to Cuba under one of the 12 OFAC (Office of Foreign Assets Control) general license categories.

Of the 12 categories of authorized travel, the most popular are:

  • Support of the Cuban People
  • Educational Activities (study abroad)
  • Family Visits
