There is no denying the country of Cuba is one of the most unique destinations in the world.

For the last 60 years international trade with Cuba has been restricted and has left this Caribbean Island
“trapped in time”. Because of this U.S. Embargo on Cuba, vintage American cars, historic untreated architecture, and the beautiful resilient Cuban culture are always surrounding you.
